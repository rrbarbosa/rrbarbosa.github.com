--- layout: default title: Rafael Ramos Regis Barbosa --- I am Brazilian born in Vila Velha, Espírito Santo. Currently, I work as a researcher at European Network for Cyber Security. I recently obtained my Ph.D degree from the University of Twente, working at the Design and Analysis of Communication Systems group. My thesis is entitled Anomaly Detection in SCADA Systems - A Network Based approach. In 2009, I received my M.Sc. in Telematics from the same University. Back in Brazil, I received my bachelor degree in 2007 from the Federal University of Espírito Santo (UFES).

I am currently investigating how to perform intrusion detection based on the observation of network-wide behaviors. My topics of interest include intrusion detection, network measurements and Industrial Control Systems.
